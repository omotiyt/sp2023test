On this page, I'd like to introduce some of my favorite places.

Since I grew up in Japan, most of my favorite places are in Japan. Here's some of them.


This is Yokohama Landmark Tower. The tower is the third tallest building and fifth tallest structure in Japan. It was near my house in Japan, so I visited quite often. There are offices, shopping centers, and a hotel inside.

This is Yamashita Park, where you can take a boat ride and have a picnic. On a fine day, you can enjoy the beautiful scenery.

This is Yokohama Chinatown. Here you can enjoy a variety of Chinese dishes at reasonable prices.

This is Yokohama Hakkeijima Sea Paradise, an amusement park consisting of an aquarium, shopping mall, hotel, marina, and amusement park. This aquarium is a bit far from where I lived, so I had to take the train, but it is a place where you can have fun all day long.
